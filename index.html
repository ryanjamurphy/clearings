The plan assumes you camp on-site and wake the kids for the darkest ~30 minutes of the night — that's the ⏰ alarm time. Prime means clear skies and a genuine Milky Way sky (moon down, full astronomical dark); a clear but moon-washed or twilight-only night is only a Maybe, since it's not worth waking them for. Around the June solstice at this latitude the sky never gets fully dark — the tool will say so.
